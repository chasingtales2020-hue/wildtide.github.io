WILD TIDE

Cardiff has one of the most powerful tidal estuaries on Earth. Amsterdam sits mostly below sea level inside a vast river delta.

Both cities are shaped by water. Both must learn to live with water in very different ways.

Personal Documentary | Feature

WE WERE FRIENDS

The story of my attempts to reunite two friends and learn about life on the way.

Michaela runs a retreat of art and healing on the North coast of The Netherlands. Jan lives in the vibrant North of Amsterdam, a storyteller retreating into his books.

As adults moving into our sixties, we now have time to reflect on the impact WWII had on our own family psyches and formed our childhoods across three countries.

Scripted Feature / Drama | In Development

PROJECT CORACLE

After their father’s sudden death, a Welsh family discover a secret barge he had been building on a landlocked canal in the mountains.

Determined to protect his memory and shield their grieving mother from its chaotic state, they restore the vessel and commit to transporting it across land and sea to Amsterdam.

Unbeknownst to them, their mother has already reinvented herself there. Project Coracle is a story about grief, absurd devotion, and the moment when love shifts from control to permission.
